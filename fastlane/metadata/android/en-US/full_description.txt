This is the a simple Android application serving the purpose of a shopping list. I modelled it after how I would use a piece of paper and kept it as simple as possible.

<b>Core features:</b>

* Simple and intuitive to use
* Dark and Light user interface mode
* No permissions required
* No tracking or sharing any data
* No ads
